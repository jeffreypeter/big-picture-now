About us

Empowering Artists to influence change globally in the lives of the most vulnerable by providing outreach and support for Service Organizations while also providing a unique income stream for up and coming Artists.

Learn more
Telling Stories

Using different Art forms to capture the inspiring lives of BigPictureNow's supported organizations through a unique story format and artistic journals.

Making Connections

Identifying with the desire for Social Justice, a need for cross-cultural understanding and the impact of global partnerships by creating more awareness about BigPictureNow's supported organizations.

Impacting Lives

Investing and aligning with the vision of these organizations through the proceeds from BigPictureNow's Art.

How we do

Partners

You work with us, not for us.

Patronage

You decide from the 80% of the sale of your work what percentage benefits the supported organization and what you take home.

Pricing

We place a favorable market value on your art based on the fair market value or current going competitive pricing.

New Clientele

We are here to help you (the artist) build a clientele base and help change the world at the same time.

No Fees

You do not pay any fees to sell your art.

360 Marketing

We take only 20% of the sale of your work to invest in top-notch marketing.

Flexibility

We give you several options to represent our Themed Story-telling Style.

Partner Organizations

An important aspect of BigPictureNow's philosophy is to inspire and encourage Leadership within our Institutions of learning by providing service opportunities through the Arts. Currently BigPictureNow partners with Campus Ambassadors At Indiana University to achieve this goal.

Campus Ambassadors

At IU is a Non-Profit organization at Indiana University which provides practical tools and resources that encourage active leadership in the immediate community and global marketplace. It desires is to create an environment that encourages Leadership that is servant-led in orientation, motivated by the life and impact of Jesus Christ of the Gospels.
